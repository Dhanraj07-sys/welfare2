About the portal

This portal brings vital welfare schemes for women into one easy place. Whether you are seeking education support, health services, livelihood opportunities, or financial assistance, our goal is to make the information clear, up‑to‑date, and truly usable—especially for beneficiaries across India.

What we offer

We focus on making the process to find and use schemes simpler. Key features include:

  • Centralized information: A curated list of active schemes with eligibility, benefits, and application steps—so you don’t need to search many different sites.
  • Practical guidance: Clear next steps, contact pointers, and simple explanations anyone can follow on phones or low‑cost devices.
  • Ongoing support: Updates on new programs or changes, plus links to local centers or helplines where available.

Our values

Accessibility: Designed for use on mobile devices, with readable fonts, soft backgrounds, and straightforward navigation.

Trust and safety: We highlight official schemes, correct terminology, and guidance sourced from public programs to help users avoid confusion or scams.

Empowerment: Information that helps women and families make decisions, plan education or work paths, and access health or financial help with confidence.

Get involved

If you’re a beneficiary, volunteer, or organization, you can help this portal grow by sharing verified scheme details, local workshops, or success stories. Together, we can help more women find the support they need—when they need it.
